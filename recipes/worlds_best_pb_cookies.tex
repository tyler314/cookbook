Ingredients
	\begin{itemize}
		\item 1 cup white sugar
		\item 1 cup brown sugar
		\item 1 cup butter
		\item 1 egg
		\item 1 cup peanut oil
		\item 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
		\item 1 cup crushed corn flakes
		\item $\frac{1}{2}$ cup shredded coconut
		\item $\frac{1}{2}$ cup chopped walnuts
		\item $3\frac{1}{2}$ cups flour
		\item 1 tsp baking soda
		\item 1 tsp salt
		\item 1 tsp vanilla
	\end{itemize}
	
	\noindent
	Directions
	\newline
	
	Cream butter \& sugars well. Add egg and mix well. Combine in the peanut oil, vanilla, rolled oats, corn flakes, coconut, \& walnuts. Sift together the flour, soda, \& salt into a homogeneous mix. Add mix to cream mixture.
	
	Form into balls the size of small walnuts. Place on ungreased cookie sheet. Flatten with fork dipped in water.
	
	Bake at 375 degrees for 12--15 minutes. Let sit a few minutes before removing from hot cookie sheet to cool on racks. Makes 3 dozen of the best cookies you have ever tasted.
